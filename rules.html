Community Rules

Welcome to the Roblox creators community! By remaining in this server, you agree to abide by these rules.

1. Respect and Politeness

Treat all members with respect, regardless of their role or experience. Harassment, insults, hate speech, and personal attacks are strictly forbidden. Be friendly and welcoming to new members.

2. Spam & Content

No spamming, flooding the chat, or excessive use of mentions. NSFW (Not Safe For Work) or explicit content is strictly prohibited. Malicious links or files are forbidden and will result in an immediate ban.

3. Advertising

Self-promotion for your Roblox creations is allowed, but only in the designated channels. Asking for or trading Robux is strictly prohibited.

4. Safety & Privacy

Do not share any personal information (real name, address, phone number, etc.). Be cautious with external links and DMs from strangers. Report any suspicious activity to staff.

5. In-Game Conduct

Fair play is mandatory. Cheating, exploits, or other malicious behavior in games is not allowed. Be a good sport during events and sessions with other members.

6. The Staff

The staff is here to enforce the rules. Please respect their decisions and instructions. For any issues, please contact a staff member directly.

7. Project Forum Rules

This is the place to showcase your game development work, prototypes, and creative ideas.

  • **Allowed posts:** Work-in-progress updates, announcements for your own games or tools, collaboration requests, and devlogs.
  • **Not allowed:** Off-topic content, low-effort or spam posts, advertising unrelated communities or servers, and sharing assets without permission.

8. Commissions Rules

This channel is exclusively for commissions. All discussions should be transparent and take place in a thread. Never accept or request money outside of secure and traceable payment methods. Never pay the full amount before the final work is delivered.
