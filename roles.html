Our Roles

Here's a list of all roles and their purpose in the server.

Admin

Manages all aspects of the server. Full authority.

Moderator

Enforces rules, assists members, and keeps the server organized.

Developer

Works on game systems, logic, and scripting.

Artist

Designs visual assets like UI, textures, icons, and concept art.

Builder

Constructs environments, maps, and structures in Roblox Studio.

Animator

Creates character animations and motion sequences for Roblox games.

Musician

Composes soundtracks, effects, and audio assets for projects.

Writer

Writes dialogue, lore, UI text, and other narrative content.

Member

Default role for members who have accepted the rules.

VIP

Special members with perks or community recognition.

Event Participant

Given to users who take part in community events.

Tester

Accesses test versions and unreleased features.
